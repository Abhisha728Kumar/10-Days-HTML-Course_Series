        DAY 05: INTRODUCTION TO LISTS

What is List?
List is the collection of items displayed in the webpage by enclosing the each <li> item in <ul> or <ol>.

<ul> stands for unordered list and <ol> stands for ordered list.

Case of using:

Case 1: Unordered list
<ul>
<li> item 1</li>
<li> item 2</li>
<li> item 3</li>
<li> item 4</li>
</ul>

Case 2: Ordered list
<ol>
<li> item 1</li>
<li> item 2</li>
<li> item 3</li>
<li> item 4</li>
</ol>
